ISO 13485:2016
Clause: 4.2.4
Title: Control of Documents

SUMMARY:
Clause 4.2.4 requires the organization to establish and maintain a controlled document management system ensuring documents are reviewed, approved, updated, and distributed appropriately. Obsolete documents must be prevented from unintended use.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Implement a version control system
- Ensure documents are reviewed and approved prior to release
- Control document distribution
- Prevent unintended use of obsolete documents
- Manage externally originated documents

MANDATORY PROCESS:
Process Name: Document Control Management
Process Description:
This process ensures QMS documents are identified, reviewed, approved, version-controlled, distributed appropriately, and protected against unintended use of obsolete versions.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Document control procedure
- External document control procedure (if separate)

REQUIRED RECORDS:
- Version history logs
- Approval records
- Distribution records
- List of external documents

EXPECTED EVIDENCE:
- Documented version tracking
- Approved document records
- Controlled access to current versions
- Identification of obsolete documents
