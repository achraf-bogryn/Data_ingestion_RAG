ISO 13485:2016
Clause: 4.1.3
Title: Monitoring and Control of Processes

SUMMARY:
Clause 4.1.3 requires the organization to define criteria and methods to ensure effective operation and control of QMS processes, including monitoring, measurement, and record retention.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Define operational criteria and control methods for processes
- Establish performance indicators (KPIs) where appropriate
- Monitor and measure processes
- Maintain records demonstrating process control

MANDATORY PROCESS:
Process Name: Process Monitoring and Performance Control
Process Description:
This process ensures the organization defines measurable criteria, monitors performance indicators, and maintains records demonstrating effective process control.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Process procedures
- Defined KPI framework
- Monitoring and measurement procedures

REQUIRED RECORDS:
- Monitoring reports
- KPI results
- Process performance records

EXPECTED EVIDENCE:
- Documented monitoring results
- KPI tracking logs
- Records demonstrating process stability and control
