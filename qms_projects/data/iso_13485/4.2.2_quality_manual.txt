ISO 13485:2016
Clause: 4.2.2
Title: Quality Manual

SUMMARY:
Clause 4.2.2 requires the organization to establish and maintain a Quality Manual defining the scope of the QMS and describing process interactions.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Establish a documented Quality Manual
- Define the QMS scope within the manual
- Justify exclusions or non-applicability where relevant
- Describe process interactions
- Reference applicable procedures

MANDATORY PROCESS:
Process Name: Quality Manual Development and Maintenance
Process Description:
This process ensures the Quality Manual accurately defines the QMS scope, structure, exclusions, and references to applicable procedures.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Version-controlled Quality Manual

REQUIRED RECORDS:
- Version history
- Approval records
- Distribution records

EXPECTED EVIDENCE:
- Approved Quality Manual
- Documented scope and exclusions
- Controlled revision tracking
