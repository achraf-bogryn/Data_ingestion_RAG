ISO 13485:2016
Clause: 4.1.2
Title: Process Approach and Risk-Based Approach

SUMMARY:
Clause 4.1.2 requires the organization to apply a process-based approach within the QMS and integrate risk-based thinking into process control and management.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Identify all processes necessary for the QMS
- Define the sequence and interaction between processes
- Apply a risk-based approach to process control
- Ensure risk management is integrated into process planning and execution

MANDATORY PROCESS:
Process Name: Process Management and Risk Integration
Process Description:
This process ensures systematic identification, mapping, and risk evaluation of QMS processes, integrating risk-based thinking into process control and monitoring.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Process mapping documentation
- Process interaction diagram
- Risk-based approach procedure

REQUIRED RECORDS:
- Process risk analyses
- Process review records

EXPECTED EVIDENCE:
- Approved process map
- Documented risk assessments per process
- Evidence of risk-based decision making
