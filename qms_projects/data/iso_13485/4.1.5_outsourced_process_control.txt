ISO 13485:2016
Clause: 4.1.5
Title: Control of Outsourced Processes

SUMMARY:
Clause 4.1.5 requires the organization to control outsourced processes that impact product conformity. Monitoring and control mechanisms must be proportionate to the associated risk.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Identify outsourced processes impacting product conformity
- Define monitoring and control measures proportionate to risk
- Formalize quality requirements in written agreements with external providers

MANDATORY PROCESS:
Process Name: Outsourced Process Control
Process Description:
This process ensures that externally provided processes are evaluated, monitored, and controlled to maintain product conformity and regulatory compliance.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Outsourcing control procedure
- Supplier quality agreements
- Contractual quality requirements

REQUIRED RECORDS:
- Supplier evaluations
- Supplier audit reports
- Monitoring and performance records

EXPECTED EVIDENCE:
- Approved supplier agreements
- Documented supplier evaluations
- Audit findings and follow-up actions
