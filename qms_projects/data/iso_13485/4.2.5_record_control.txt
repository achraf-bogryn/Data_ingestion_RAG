ISO 13485:2016
Clause: 4.2.5
Title: Control of Records

SUMMARY:
Clause 4.2.5 requires the organization to control QMS records to ensure their identification, storage, protection, retrieval, retention, and controlled disposal.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Define retention periods for records
- Define storage and archiving methods
- Ensure integrity, confidentiality, and accessibility
- Define controlled destruction procedures

MANDATORY PROCESS:
Process Name: Record Control and Retention Management
Process Description:
This process ensures QMS records are properly identified, securely stored, retained for defined periods, protected against loss or alteration, and destroyed in a controlled manner when retention periods expire.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Record control procedure
- Retention policy

REQUIRED RECORDS:
- Retention register
- Access logs
- Archiving records
- Destruction records

EXPECTED EVIDENCE:
- Defined retention schedule
- Controlled record storage system
- Evidence of secure destruction
- Traceability of archived records
