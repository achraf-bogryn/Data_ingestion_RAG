ISO 13485:2016
Clause: 4.1.4
Title: Control of Changes to the QMS and Product

SUMMARY:
Clause 4.1.4 requires the organization to control changes affecting the Quality Management System (QMS) or medical device conformity. All changes must be identified, evaluated, approved, and implemented in a controlled manner.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Identify proposed changes affecting the QMS
- Evaluate the impact of changes on the QMS and on the medical device/product
- Approve changes prior to implementation
- Ensure controlled implementation of approved changes

MANDATORY PROCESS:
Process Name: QMS Change Management and Control
Process Description:
This process ensures that any change affecting the QMS or product conformity is formally assessed, approved, documented, and implemented under controlled conditions.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Change management procedure

REQUIRED RECORDS:
- Change register
- Approved impact assessment records
- Change approval documentation

EXPECTED EVIDENCE:
- Documented impact analyses
- Approved change requests
- Traceability of implemented changes
